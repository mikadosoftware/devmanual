Network Monitoring¶

historical note

This area has moved on considerably as well. netflow is still the heart of
it and RRTD graphs exist everywhere still but graphite and the new PacketBeat are showing a new wave.

Introduction¶

Monitoring what is flowing over your network tubes is more important than ever. For reasons of capacity planning, as well as security and “getting a feel for the network” good tools matter.
